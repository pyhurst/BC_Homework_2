About me

Hello! I was born and raised in the suburbs of Chicago. I had a knack for numbers at an early age, which led me into the field of Finance at the Illinois State University. After graduation, I worked as a stock broker and then specialized in day trading the Euro for 6 years. I loved what I did, but realized that I did not want to trade the financial markets for the rest of my life so I applied for the Northwestern University Coding Bootcamp where I learned to be a full stack developer.
